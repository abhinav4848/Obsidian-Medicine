If [[anaemia|ferritin]] Low: Give Iron Supplements
- Treating iron deficiency often improves RLS even if Hb normal. 

If Ferritin Normal: 
- Give Dopamine Agonists (Non-ergot ones like: Pramipexole or Ropinirole -> also used in [[parkinson's disease|Parkinson's]]). 
- Alternatives: (gabapentin, pregabalin)-> They are alpha-2-delta calcium channel modulators.
