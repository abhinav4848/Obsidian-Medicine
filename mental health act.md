[Mental Health Act Made Simple (Most Commonly Used Sections)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=W7E_EaXWvgw) on YouTube.

Mental health acts are for mental health disorders only and cannot be used to support an admission to a general hospital for treatment of a physical health condition such as pneumonia.

*AMHP: Approved mental health professional*
*NR: nearest relative*
