# Symptoms
Persistent intrusive flashbacks, vivid memories and recurring dreams + difficulty sleeping, irritability, poor concentration, hypervigilance and exaggerated startle response.

# Treatments
- Offer trauma-focused CBT if they have presented > 1 month after a traumatic event.
- Offer EMDR (Eye movement desensitisation and reprocessing) 
	- 1-3 months after non-combat-related trauma if the person has a preference for EMDR, and
	- to all adults 3+ months after non-combat-related trauma. 
	- *The reason is that everyone is expected to have some psychological disturbance after combat, so the waiting period before diagnosing PTSD in them is longer (3m instead of 1m)*
- Offer supported trauma-focused computerised CBT for adults who have presented more than 3 months after a traumatic event if they prefer it to f2f trauma-focused CBT or EMDR, as long as they don't have severe PTSD symptoms, in particular dissociative symptoms, and they are not at risk of harm to themselves or others.
- Psychologically-focused debriefing may worsen the problem and is not recommended.
