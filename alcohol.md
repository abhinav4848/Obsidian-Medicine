# Wernicke Encephalopathy
Triad: #triad - Ophthalmoplegia, Ataxia, Confusion. 
Due to B1 deficiency. 

- Oculomotor dysfunction:
	- Ophthalmoplegia: lateral rectus palsy, conjugate gaze palsy
	- Nystagmus
- Gait Ataxia 
- Encephalopathy:
	- Confusion, disorientation, indifference, and inattentiveness
- Peripheral sensory neuropathy 

Give Pabrinex which has Vit B1 & Vit C.

**Else**: Wernicke-Korsakoff
- Amnesia (anterograde & Retrograde)
- Confabulation
