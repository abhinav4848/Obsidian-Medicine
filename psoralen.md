# Psoralen
Photosensitising agent. Makes skin more sensitive to effect of UVA rays.
## Use
PUVA= Psoralen + UVA
- PUVA is used to treat [[plaque psoriasis]].
## Complication
can lead to [[cancer- skin|squamous cell carcinoma of skin]]. 
- This is due to the mutagenic effect of UVA radiation on keratinocytes when used in conjunction with psoralen. The risk is particularly high in patients who have received a high cumulative dose of PUVA or those who have fair skin.
