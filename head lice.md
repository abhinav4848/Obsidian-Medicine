If live head louse is found in a family member, advise detection combing for all contacts. 
# Investigation
Detection combing is done on wet or dry hair with a special detection comb. 
# Management
- Only initiate treatment if when live louse is found. Prophylactic treatment is not recommended. 
- Wet combing is a method of treatment for confirmed head lice infestation, involving systematically combing the hair over a 2-week period to remove lice.
