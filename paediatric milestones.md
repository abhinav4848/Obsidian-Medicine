Social
- smile: 2 months
- eye contact 3 months

Fine motor
- hold objects: 5 months
- reach for objects: 6 months
- Transfer objects b/w hands: 9 months

Reach for something (3) pass it to your other hand (6) point to something else (9) and pincer pick it up (12), Place the two objects in a tower (15m)  
Add another to make it three (18m)  
2x the height at 2y (tower of 6 at 2y)  
Add another 3 at 3yo (tower of 9)

Gross Motor
- Sit unsupported: 12 months (no worries about standing unsupported)
- Walk: 18 months
- Run: 30 months (2.5 years)

Verbal
- At least single meaningful word: 18 months (else refer for hearing assessment)
- Sentence with 2-3 words: 30 months (2.5 years)
