# Goals
#### 1 year
**Gross**: To walk
**Fine**: To have a controlled pincer grasp
**Verbal**: To say one word with meaning (i.e. the child says the word with intent and it means the same thing every time)

---
# Gross Motor
- Sit unsupported: 6 months (no worries about standing unsupported)
- 12m: Walks up & cuts his first birthday cake on his feet. 
- Walk: 18 months
- Run: 30 months (2.5 years)

# Fine motor
aka hand eye coordination. 
#mnemonic Learn it as a movement 

| Fine motor / hand skill                                           | Age                     |
| ----------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------- |
| Reaches for an object                                             | **3 months**            |
| Holds it in palmar grasp and passes it to the other hand          | **6 months**            |
| Points to objects                                                 | **9 months**            |
| Uses pincer grasp to pick up small objects                        | **12 months**           |
| Place the two objects in a tower (Builds a tower of **2** blocks) | **15 months**           |
| Add another to make it three (Builds a tower of **3** blocks)     | **18 months**           |
| 2x the height at 2y (Builds a tower of **6** blocks)              | **24 months (2 years)** |
| Add another 3 at 3yo (Builds a tower of **9** blocks)             | **36 months (3 years)** |

# Language
Includes hearing and speaking. 
- At least single meaningful word: 18 months (else refer for hearing assessment)
- Sentence with 2-3 words: 30 months (2.5 years)
