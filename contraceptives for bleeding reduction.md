# Sickle cell disease + Menorrhagia
[[contraceptives|Depo Provera]] (DMPA)
POP & IUS are next choice for SCD.
Don't give COCP/IUD in SCD.

# Migraine with Aura
- IUCD & barrier methods (UKMEC 1)
- Then consider POP, IUS, DMPA (UKMEC 2)

# h/o VTE
- H/o VTE only: IUCD > IUS
- H/o VTE + menorrhagia: IUS

# Sexually active + Menorrhagia/Dysmenorrhea
- Fibroid not distorting Uterine cavity (& <3cm size):
	- Mirena (> 20yo + Needs long term contraception)-> it shrinks fibroid, reduces bleed
	- Ulipristal acetate up to 4 courses especially pre surgery if size > 3cm.
	- Endometrial ablation for fibroids < 3cm.
- Fibroid distorting Uterine cavity:
	- COCP/POP/Implants (< 20, no long contraception needed, no c/I to COCP)
	- Implant e.g. Nexplanon (Fibroid distorting uterine cavity)
