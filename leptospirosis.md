# Clinical features
Red eyes (Subconjunctival Haemorrhage),
Followed by Yellow eyes (Jaundice) + Rash
Others: Fever, rigors, malaise, Arthralgia, Myalgia
# Investigation:
To confirm Dx: Serology >
Just diagnosis: Blood (first 7-10 days) and Urine (7-30 days) culture and sensitivity
# Treatment
Usually mild & self limited > Doxycycline (for mild cases) > Ampicillin/Benzylpenicillin (for severe cases)
