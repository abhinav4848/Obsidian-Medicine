# Seronegative arthritis
It's a group of [[spondyloarthritis]] conditions. 

Features: 
- Lack of Rheumatoid factor & Anti-CCP (seronegative)
- [[hla-b27|HLA-B27]] association. 

Group includes
1. Predominantly Axial:
	1. [[Ankylosing spondylitis]]: Anterior [[uveitis]], [[foot problems|Achilles tendonitis]] 
	2. non-radiographic axial spondyloarthritis
2. Predominantly Peripheral:
	1. [[Reactive arthritis]]: [[Conjunctivitis]], [[dactylitis]]
	2. [[behcet's disease]] 
	3. Enteropathic Spondyloarthritis: Arthritis that accompanies [[inflammatory bowel disease|IBD]]
	4. [[whipple disease]] 
	5. [[psoriatic arthritis]]

# Investigation
See [[antibodies]]. 
First test: do RF  & ANA (for connective tissue disease)
# Treatments
**NSAID**: Rheumatoid arthritis, Ankylosing spondylitis, Reactive arthritis, [[Gout]].
**Steroid**: Sarcoidosis, Churg-Strauss, Wegener, GCA, Polymyositis, Dermatomyositis, Polymyalgia rheumatica.
