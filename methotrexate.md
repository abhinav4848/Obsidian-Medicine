It's a [[rheumatoid arthritis|DMARD]]- [[rheumatoid arthritis]], [[psoriasis]], chemotherapy

The drug is taken weekly. Not daily.
Patients also take [[folic acid]] acid on a different day of the week than MTX

## Monitoring
Repeat these weekly till therapy stabilised, then every 2-3 months. 
- FBC (It's an [[folic acid|anti-folate]]), 
- U&e, 
- LFT (metabolized in the liver).
## Side Effects
- Mucositis
- Myelosuppression
- Liver fibrosis.  
- Pneumonitis: Can cause dry cough (like ACE inhibitor) because MTX can lead to [[interstitial lung disease vs pneumonitis|pneumonitis]]. 

>*New answer is Pneumonitis, but if not in option, choose [[lung fibrosis|pulmonary fibrosis]].
>- [[Leflunomide]] and Sulfasalazine cause [[interstitial lung disease vs pneumonitis|ILD]]. 
>- MTX is more associated with pneumonitis.*
## Interactions
- 🚩avoid prescribing [[trimethoprim]] or [[co-trimoxazole]] concurrently - increases risk of marrow aplasia
- high-dose aspirin increases the risk of methotrexate toxicity secondary to reduced excretion
## Toxicity
Methotrexate toxicity: Treatment of choice is Folinic acid
# Pregnancy
Can cause abortion in [[ectopic pregnancy]].
Methotrexate & Trimethoprim are [[folic acid|anti-folate]].
- Men & Women to avoid pregnancy for ==6 months== after treatment has stopped.
