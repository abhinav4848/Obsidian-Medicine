Serotonin is synthesized from Tryptophan. 
High concentration of Serotonin is found in Intestine, platelets, brain.

There are 7 types of serotonin receptors (1-7). All of them G-protein coupled except 5HT3 which is ligand gated ion channel.

| 5HT2     | Platelets, Smooth muscles, cerebral cortex (5HT2A)<br>Fundus of stomach (5HT2B),<br>Choroid (5HT2C) | Platelet aggregation<br>contraction of smooth muscles<br>Activation of neurons<br><br>Fundus- contraction<br>Choroid- CSF production          | All are 5HT2A antagonists:<br>Ketanserin, Cyproheptadine, Methysergide, [[antipsychotic drugs#Atypical\|Atypical antipsychotics]]                       |
