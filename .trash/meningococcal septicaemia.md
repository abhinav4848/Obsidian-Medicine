Meningococcal septicaemia is a frightening condition for patients, parents and doctors. It is associated with a high morbidity and mortality unless treated early - meningococcal disease is the leading infectious cause of death in early childhood. A high index of suspicion is therefore needed. 

# Management if suspected meningococcal septicaemia 
- give intramuscular or intravenous [[benzylpenicillin]] unless there is a history of anaphylaxis (do not give if this will delay hospital transfer)
- NICE recommend phoning 999
