**Multiforme**: Having more than one shape or appearance. (mnemonic: Multi causes)

Lesion is due to immune complex deposition. H/o sore throat followed by fever, myalgia, lethargy.

Target lesions. Vesicle surrounded by hemorrhagic maculopapule.

Dusky red blistering center with surrounding pale area

Initially seen on hands/feet before spreading to trunk. Spreads towards center.

Weeping and crusting painful lesion

Causes:

- HSV > [Mycoplasma](onenote:#Respiratory&section-id={210C3954-BE8B-A24C-8E7F-F1B991349700}&page-id={5EDD7540-FF15-4803-B1E9-BBDDE7E91E20}&object-id={698FD5FD-1771-4CF1-BE39-7B38D364831E}&2B&base-path=https://d.docs.live.net/450c0e1b0b9c7922/Documents/Onenote/MBBS/PLAB%201%20+%20MSRA.one), Strep sore throat.

- NSAID/Nevirapine,
- Penicillin (Amoxicillin)/Allopurinol,
- COCP/Carbamazepine.
