Coxsackie, Enterovirus.
Highly infectious. 

Painful ulcers on tongue, hard palate, buccal mucosa.
- Oral lesions resolve without treatment in 5-7 days. But they can affect oral intake. 
- Grey blisters on hands and feet. They crust and gradually disappear over 5-10 days without scarring
Can attend [[school in children with rash|school]]. 
# Treatment
Symptomatic treatment.
# Complication
viral meningitis & encephalitis
