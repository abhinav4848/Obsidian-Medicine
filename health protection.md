Public health is included in the RCGP curriculum under the statement on 'Healthy People: promoting health and preventing disease.'  
  
Health protection involves guarding the public against threats to health, for example, legislation on air pollution or vaccination programs against infectious diseases. The Food Standards Agency (FSA) is a Health Protection body - it is a Government agency responsible for food safety. Environmental Health Officers from the FSA inspect premises providing food to the public to ensure its hygiene and safety.
