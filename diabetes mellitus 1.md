# Test for T1DM %%transcluded%%
- Islet autoantibody profile to assess whether the patient is an early T1DM. More the antibodies, means more the likelihood of Type 1 DM. 
	- **Anti-GAD:** Glutamic Acid Decarboxylase Autoantibodies
	- **Anti-IA2**: Insulinoma-Associated-2 Autoantibodies
	- **Anti-ZnT8**: Zinc Transporter-8 Autoantibodies
	- *ICA is not specific for T1DM & not used for screening. IAA is another antibody that can be used, but advice from specialist is only to do above 3.*
