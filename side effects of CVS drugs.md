**BB**: Asthma
**CCB**: Edema, Gingival hypertrophy

**Loop diuretic**
Hypo NaBpK, HyperU

**Thiazide:**
HypoNaBpK (Hyponatremia, Hypotension, [[Hypokalemia]])
Hyper GLUC (glycemia, lipidemia, uricemia, [[hypercalcemia|calcemia]]) #mnemonic 
Metabolic Alkalosis
Impotence

**K+ sparing diuretic**
Hyponatremia, hyperkalemia, gynecomastia
