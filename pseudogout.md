Calcium pyrophosphate.
# Cause:
Hyperparathyroidism, hemochromatosis, Hypophosphatemia, Hypomagnesemia, Hypothyroidism, 

# Clinical Features
Joint pain, swelling, erythema, warmth, fever, raised WBC. 
Affects knee > Wrist, shoulder, ankle.
- Can affect both shoulders. Either at once (mono-), or at same time (poly-). 
- Pain doesn't ease with passive motion as it's intra-articular pathology. 

# Investigation
Positive birefringent crystals.
