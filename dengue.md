h/o [[travel infections|travel]] to/from Far East Asia (e.g. Indonesia, Bangkok)
Short incubation- 3-14 days. 
# Clinical features:
Biphasic Fever
headache, 
retro-orbital pain, 
Generalized Rash #rash 
	comes on as the fever reduces
	exanthem (aka widespread rash) that are confluent, with islands of sparing. [dermnetz](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/exanthems)
Myalgia, 
Tender cervical LNE.

Tourniquet test +ve #investigation
