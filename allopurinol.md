Used as long-term management of [[gout]]. 
It's an enzyme [[p450|inhibitor]]. 

Allopurinol reduces clearance of 
1. 🚩[[Azathioprine]] (prodrug of 6-MP) clearance -> Immunosuppression -> Neutropenic sepsis
2. Cyclophosphamide -> Marrow toxicity
3. Theophylline

🚩[[ace inhibitors|ACE inhibitors]] (Captopril, enalapril, Lisinopril, Ramipril), Thiazide, Indapamide
