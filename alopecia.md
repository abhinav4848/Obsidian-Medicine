# Alopecia Areata
Localised well demarcated patches of hair loss.
At the edges of the hair loss may be small broken, exclamation mark hairs.
Hair regrows eventually. 

# Treatment
- topical or intralesional corticosteroids
- topical minoxidil
- phototherapy
- dithranol
- contact immunotherapy
- wigs
