## Why do we use it?
Think of PV as an alternative to ESR, with some advantages.
PV is not specific for rheumatological conditions and there are many other conditions that can lead to a raised PV level.

## Why PV can be normal when ESR/CRP are not (and vice versa)
- **CRP** rises fast and falls fast
- **ESR** depends on RBC properties
- **PV** reflects **overall protein load**, so:
    - Less day-to-day variation
    - Better for chronic inflammation
