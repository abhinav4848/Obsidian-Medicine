# Exciting (Stimulant)

| ---------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Cocaine                      | Classic cause of acute chest pain in young adults. Cocaine causes coronary vasospasm, increased heart rate, and blood pressure, which can lead to angina or MI even in otherwise healthy young people.<br><br>Cocaine is not an opioid -> it blocks the uptake of dopamine, NA, Serotonin.<br><br>**Adverse Effect**<br>1. **Cardiovascular**: coronary artery spasm → myocardial ischemia/infarction, both tachycardia and bradycardia may occur, hypertension, QRS widening and [[qt prolongation]], aortic dissection.<br>2. **Neurological**: seizures, mydriasis, hypertonia, hyperreflexia.<br>3. **Psychiatric Effects**: agitation, psychosis, hallucinations.<br>4. **Other Effects**: Ischaemic colitis is recognized in patients following cocaine ingestion. This should be considered if patients complain of abdominal pain or rectal bleeding, hyperthermia, metabolic acidosis, rhabdomyolysis, metabolic acidosis, hyperthermia<br><br>**Treatment**<br>[[benzodiazepines\|diazepam]] is first line for most problems.<br>- Chest pain: IV benzodiazepines + nitrates, PCI if MI<br>- HTN: BDZ + Na Nitroprusside |
| Amphetamine / MDMA (Ecstasy) | Can cause tachycardia, hyperthermia, and sometimes chest discomfort, but less commonly true MI.
