## When to consider cataract?
- If there is a h/o Steroid intake (e.g. [[Asthma]], [[COPD]], RA).
- Glare at night, Dazzling (halos) around lights. (also in [[glaucoma- acute angle closure|acute angle glaucoma]]) #confusion , 
- Frequent change spectacles.
- Exposure to significant amounts of ultra violet light.
- Reduced colour vision.
# Fundoscopy
- Absent red reflex, or a shadow is visible within the red reflex
- Difficulty visualising the fundus.
