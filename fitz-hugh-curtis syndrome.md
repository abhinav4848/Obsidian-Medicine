Complication of [[gynaecological infections#Pelvic Inflammatory disease|PID]] in which the liver capsule becomes inflamed causing RUQ pain. This leads to scar tissue formation and peri hepatic adhesions. It usually occurs in women who have either [[chlamydia]] or [[gonorrhoea]].  
# Treatment
Eradicate the organism although laparoscopy is required in some patients to lyse adhesions that have formed.
