# DDx: BRVO
**Branch retinal vein occlusion** (BRVO): This occurs when a vein in the distal retinal venous system is occluded and is thought to occur due to blockage of retinal veins at arteriovenous crossings. It results in a more limited area of the fundus being affected. 
# Treatment
Indications for treatment in patients with CRVO include:
- Macular oedema- intravitreal anti-VEGF (anti-vascular endothelial growth factor) agents
- Retinal neovascularization- laser photocoagulation
