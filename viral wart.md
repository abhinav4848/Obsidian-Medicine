Warts are common and do not require treatment unless painful, unsightly or a patient is requesting treatment for a persistent wart. 

They are contagious and can easily spread. 
If on hand, more common to spread. 

# Treatment
Treatment mostly not required. 
- Most of them will resolve spontaneously within months or at most within 2 years. 
- In adults it can take up to 10 years.
- No treatment if not causing any problems. 

Topical salicylic acid

Cryotherapy 
- Children won't tolerate if too young.
