Melasma: Hyperpigmented macules in sun-exposed areas, esp face. 
Chloasma: Melasma during pregnancy. 
  
# Epidemiology  
- more common in women
- more common in people with darker skin
# Causes 
- pregnancy
- [[COCP]], hormone replacement therapy
	- The risk of melasma is associated with duration of combined pill use.
# Treatment
- Wear SPF cream daily. 
- hydroquinone 4%
- Change [[cocp|COCP]] to POP as oestrogen is thought to be main culprit.
