Following ovulation induction, women can get OHSS- symptoms are Nausea/vomiting/diarrhoea/abdo pain. 

# Management
Most women with mild or moderate OHSS can be managed as an out-patient but should be managed by a team with specialist knowledge of OHSS.

Hospital admission should be considered for women who are unable to achieve satisfactory pain control, are unable to maintain adequate fluid intake due to nausea, show signs of worsening OHSS despite outpatient intervention, are unable to attend for regular outpatient follow-up or have critical OHSS.

All fertility centres should provide women undergoing fertility treatment with a contact number for advice and assessment should they develop any possible symptoms of OHSS.
