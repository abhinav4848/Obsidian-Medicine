1. Dermatophytosis (Tinea)- superficial fungal infection of keratinised tissues by Trichophyton/Microsporum/Epidermophyton 
	1. [[tinea capitis]] (scalp)
	2. tinea barbae (beard)
	3. tinea faciei (non-bearded face)
	4. [[tinea corporis]] (trunk/limbs)
	5. tinea cruris (groin) 
	6. tinea pedis (feet)
	7. tinea manuum (hands) 
	8. tinea unguium ([[Onychomycosis]])- Fungal Nail Infection
