**At birth**
- BCG / hepatitis B vaccine if risk factors (refer below)

**2 months**
- '6-1 vaccine'
	- DPT, polio, Hib and hepatitis B
- Oral rotavirus vaccine
- PCV
- Men B

**3 months**
- '6-1 vaccine'
	- DPT, polio, Hib and hepatitis B
- Oral rotavirus vaccineÂ 

**4 months**
- '6-1 vaccine'
	- DPT, polio, Hib and hepatitis B
- PCV
- Men B

**12-13 months**
- Hib or Men C
- MMR
- PCV
- Men B

**2-8 years**
- Flu vaccine (annual)

**3-4 years**
- '4-in-1 pre-school booster'
	- DPT and polio
- MMR

**12-13 years**
- HPV vaccination for girls

**13-18 years**
- '3-in-1 teenage booster'
	- DT and polio
- Men ACWY
