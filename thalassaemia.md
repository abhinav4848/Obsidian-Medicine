[[autosomal inheritance|autosomal recessive]]
- Carrier status is important for genetic counselling, as two carriers have a risk of having a child with beta-thalassaemia major.

# beta thalassemia trait
Causes mild hypochromic, microcytic anaemia.
### Features 
- Individuals are usually asymptomatic -> identified incidentally, or during family/antenatal screening. 
- Mild hypochromic, microcytic anaemia
- Microcytosis is characteristically disproportionate to the degree of anaemia
- HbA2 raised (> 3.5%)
- Normal or slightly increased HbF
