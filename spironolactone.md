# Indications
- [[Ascites]] in [[cirrhosis]]. 
- [[hypertension|HTN]] (used as step 4 in treatment. One of the last options)
- [[Heart failure]]
- Nephrotic syndrome

# HTN control
Start Spironolactone 12.5mg OD, and check U&E after 2 weeks. If stable, increase to 25mg OD
