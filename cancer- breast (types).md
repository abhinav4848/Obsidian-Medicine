# Approach to classification
Think of breast cancer this way:
1. Where is the tumour relative to the basement membrane?
	1. In situ (non-invasive)- Cancer cells are confined above the basement membrane, Can't metastasize, but can progress to invasive. 
	2. Invasive- Cancer cells have breached the basement membrane & can metastasize
2. Where did it come from?
	1. Lactiferous Ducts (most common)
	2. Lobules
3. Special clinical patterns
