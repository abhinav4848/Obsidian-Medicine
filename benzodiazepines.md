Benzodiazepines act by enhancing the effect of gamma-aminobutyric acid (GABA) in the brain, which leads to 
- sedative, 
- hypnotic (sleep-inducing),
- anxiolytic (anti-anxiety),
- anticonvulsant, and 
- muscle relaxant properties.
