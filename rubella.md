Initial prodrome: Fever, conjunctivitis, URTi, Tender post-auricular LNE.

[[Rash]]
- Transient pink/light red maculopapular rash 
- Initially on face, starts behind ear, before spreading to whole body  
- Forchheimer [[spots]] on soft palate + LNE #named 
# Treatment
Immunisation is essential as there's no treatment. 
Support + PCT
# Complication
In pregnant women, teratogenic. 
- High Risk before 16 weeks
- Minimal risk of deafness only between 16- 20 weeks. 
- No documented risk after 20 weeks of gestation.
