Chondrodermatitis nodularis helicis (CDNH) is a benign, painful condition that affects the cartilaginous portion of the ear.
- Inflammation of both the cartilage (chondro-) and of the skin (-dermatitis) causing a bump (a nodule, hence nodularis).
![CNH](https://www.pcds.org.uk/imager/gallery/clinical/chondermatitis-nodularis-helicis/13033/CDNH1_2615addf7111d83624b7a7a5f3466fb3.jpg)

# Aetiology
- Persistent pressure on the ear- Sleep on the same side/headset pressure, 
- trauma, 
- exposure to cold. 
- More in men, older age. 
# Clinical Features
It can be very painful, especially when touched, but is otherwise harmless.
# Treatment
It usually clears in a few months but can come back. 
collagen injection for cushioning, steroid injection for pain, surgical excision for refractory cases
