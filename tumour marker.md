There are 4 types of tumour markers:
- MABs against carbohydrate or glycoprotein tumour antigens
	- All tumour markers beginning with CA are monoclonal antibodies.
- Tumour antigens
- Enzymes (ALP, Neurone specific enolase)
- Hormones (calcitonin, ADH...)
