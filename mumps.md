Transmit by saliva droplets, close contact

**POMP**: Parotitis, orchitis, [[meningitis]], [[pancreatitis]]. #mnemonic 
-> starts u/l then becomes b/l tender painful swelling at angle of jaw (peri-auricular)
-> fever, dry mouth (due to block of salivary gland), difficult to open mouth (due to swelling)
# Treatment
PCT/Ibuprofen + Reassure
