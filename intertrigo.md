Intertrigo is an inflammatory condition of the skin folds caused by 
- skin-on-skin friction. 
Secondary infection with bacterial or fungal infection is common. 

Sites: 
- Groin, axillae, and inframammary folds. 
- Also antecubital fossae; umbilical, perineal, or interdigital areas; neck creases; and folds of the eyelids.

# Investigation
- If a secondary infection is suspected a swab should be taken for MC&S
- If there is scale, skin scrapings should be sent for mycology to look for tinea

# Management
### General measures
Minimise friction, lose weight, shower after exercise. 
Wearing open toe shoes will protect from toe web intertrigo. 
Avoid absorptive powders like talc & cornstarch which may irritate skin. 
### Medical
Daktacort cream BD (Miconazole -> Treats candida & some Gram +ve bacteria)
