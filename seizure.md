# Drugs lowering Seizure threshold
- All [[SSRI]]s
- atypical [[antipsychotic drugs|antipsychotics]] lower seizure threshold more than typical. 
- [[ciprofloxacin]] 
# Acute Management
For seizures lasting >=5 mins
#### Rectal diazepam (dose repeated once after 5-10mins) 
- Neonate (1 month): 1.25-2.5mg 
- < 12yo: 5mg
- > 12yo: 10mg
#### Midazolam oromucosal
buccal administration
