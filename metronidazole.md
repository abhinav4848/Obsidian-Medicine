Antibiotic active against 
- anaerobic bacteria (only works in low Oxygen environments i.e. below the diaphragm)
- Some protozoa (giardia, entamoeba, trichomonas)
# Uses
- Used in [[gynaecological infections|bacterial vaginosis]] & [[gynaecological infections|trichomoniasis]]. 
- Used in [[acne rosacea]].
- Used for perianal fistulae in [[inflammatory bowel disease|Crohn's disease]]. 
- A [[disulfiram]] like reaction can occur if metro & [[alcohol]] are taken together.
- Life threatening [[c. difficile]]. 
# Side Effects
- It’s an [[p450|enzyme inhibitor]] and will increase effectiveness of OCP, warfarin.
- Reduces renal excretion of [[Lithium]] and can cause toxicity.
- Can cause [[colour & vision changes in body|Optic Neuritis]] & [[seizure|seizures]]. 

Rarely it can lead to neurotoxic effects, including 
- peripheral neuropathy (long term use)
- ototoxicity, 
- encephalopathy,
