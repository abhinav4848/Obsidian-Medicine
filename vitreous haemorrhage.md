Typically presents with the sudden onset appearance of spots or floaters, blurring of vision or [[loss of vision|visual loss]]. It results from retinal haemorrhage into the vitreous. 

Proliferative [[retinopathy|diabetic retinopathy]] is a risk factor due to the growth of abnormal blood vessels in the retina that is susceptible to bleeding.

(For exam, Diabetes + vision loss = vitreous haemorrhage) #important
