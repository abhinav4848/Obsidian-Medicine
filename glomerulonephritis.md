# IgA Glomerulonephritis
aka Berger's disease (Not [[vascular ulcers|Buerger's disease]] which is thromboangiitis obliterans), Syn-pharyngitic glomerulonephritis

1-2 days after URTI (or along with URTI)
[[haematuria]], Young males. (HSP affects children more)

*Associated with [[hsp vs itp|HSP]]*
# Post strep (post infectious) Glomerulonephritis
1-2 weeks after, Proteinuria, Decreased C3, Renal biopsy shows humps. -> Edema, Haematuria, Proteinuria, HTN, AKI

Organism: Group A beta haemolytic streptococci (Streptococci pyogenes)

Rx: supportive
