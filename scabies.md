Caused by Sarcoptes scabiei (ixodes tick carries [[lyme disease|Borrelia burgdorferi]])

Flexor surface.
Contagious.
Face not involved.    
# Treatment
1st line: Permethrin topical 5% (whole family) applied to whole body except head >
Lyclear 5% dermal cream- adults will need 2 tubes. Children 1 tube should be fine.

2nd line: Malathion 0.5%
Permethrin can be used in pregnancy as well.
