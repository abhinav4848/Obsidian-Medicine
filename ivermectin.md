Anti-Parasite. 

It has strong anti-inflammatory property:
- It reduces TLR-2 signalling (Toll-like Receptor)
- Decreases inflammatory cytokines (IL-8, IL-1Î²)
- Calms neutrophil-driven inflammation

In [[acne rosacea]], it not only reduces inflammation, it also reduces the demodex mites that exacerbate the inflammation of rosacea.
