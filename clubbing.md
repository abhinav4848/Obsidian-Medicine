The causes of clubbing may be divided into cardiac, respiratory and other  
# Cardiac causes  
- [[congenital cyanotic diseases of heart]] (Fallot's, TGA)
- bacterial endocarditis
- atrial myxoma
# Respiratory causes  
- lung cancer
- pyogenic conditions: [[cystic fibrosis]], [[bronchiectasis]], abscess, empyema
- tuberculosis
- asbestosis, mesothelioma
- fibrosing alveolitis 
- (*COPD doesn't cause clubbing*)
# Other causes  
- Crohn's, to a lesser extent UC
- cirrhosis, [[primary biliary cholangitis & primary sclerosing cholangitis|primary biliary cirrhosis]]
- Graves' disease (thyroid acropachy)
- rare: Whipple's disease
