Clinical pathways should be used for patients presenting with symptoms potentially associated with any of the following conditions: 
1. Uncomplicated urinary tract infections (UTIs) in women  
2. Shingles  
3. Impetigo  
4. Infected insect bites  
5. Sinusitis  
6. Sore throat 
7. Acute otitis media
