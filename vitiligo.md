Common in someone with other autoimmune disease like
- T1DM,
- Addison's,
- Pernicious anaemia,
- Thyroid disease (Hashimoto),
- alopecia areata

Koebner phenomenon


Prevent: Daily sunscreen to affected areas.  
OD potent topical steroid application for an initial period of upto 2 months.
