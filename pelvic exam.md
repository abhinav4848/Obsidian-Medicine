- Pederson's speculum: can be regular length or a long one. 
- Grave's speculum/Duckbill speculum: can be regular/long/open sided.
- Cusco speculum: common in clinics
- Sim's speculum: used to assess the anterior and posterior vaginal walls. Using a bivalve speculum enables visualisation of the cervix but is less useful for identifying anterior/posterior compartment prolapse
