For older patients at risk of falling, multifactorial intervention programmes that involve 
- strength and balance training, 
- home hazard assessment and intervention, 
- vision assessment and referral, and 
- medication review are recommended. 
Brisk walking, cognitive behavioural therapy, hip protectors and vitamin D all have insufficient evidence to demonstrate any benefit.
