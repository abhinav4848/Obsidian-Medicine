**Rusty red sputum**: 
- pneumococcal pneumonia

**Yellow/green (purulent)**
- Lung abscess
- bronchiectasis
- [[cystic fibrosis]]
- pneumonia

**Clear, grey-white(mucoid)** 
- Asthma
- chronic bronchitis

**Clear, frothy pink, watery**
- Acute pulmonary edema
