Used in [[copd|COPD]]. 

| MRC scale <br>(used by NICE) | mMRC scale<br>(used by GOLD) | Degree of breathlessness related to activities                                                                                 |
| ---------------------------- | ---------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 1                            | 0                            | Not troubled by breathlessness except on strenuous exercise                                                                    |
| 2                            | 1                            | Short of breath when hurrying or walking up a slight hill                                                                      |
| 3                            | 2                            | Walks slower than contemporaries on level ground because of breathlessness, or has to stop for breath when walking at own pace |
| 4                            | 3                            | Stops for breath after walking about 100 metres or after a few minutes on level ground                                         |
| 5                            | 4                            | Too breathless to leave the house, or breathless when dressing or undressing                                                   |
