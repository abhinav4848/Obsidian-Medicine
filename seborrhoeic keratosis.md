Benign epidermal skin lesions seen in older people.
Waxy surface, stuck-on appearance. 

![Seb K|300](https://www.pcds.org.uk/imager/gallery/clinical/seborrhoeic-keratosis-syn-seborrhoeic-wart-basal-cell-papilloma/13800/SK2_fee391183f15cb4d62773032fe0be92d.jpg)

The sudden onset of multiple seborrhoeic keratoses may indicate an underlying malignancy, known as the ==Leser–Trélat sign== #named. It is thought to result from tumour-derived growth factors, such as epidermal growth factor and transforming growth factor-α, stimulating keratinocyte proliferation.

# Treatment
Can ask for treatment if it catches or bleeds. 
Can't do anything to remove or prevent more from coming.
