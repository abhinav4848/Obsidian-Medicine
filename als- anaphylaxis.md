Can give adrenaline 1:1000 1mg/ml in anterolateral aspect of mid-thing. 
< 6 months: 100-150mcg
6m-6y: 150mcg
6y-12y: 300mcg
12 years and above: 500mcg.

Give patient 2 auto injectors & train them on use.
