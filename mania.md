# Clinical Features
1. [[psych terminology|Flight of Ideas]] is characteristic of mania.
2. [[psych terminology|Clang association]]. 
3. Pressured speech (fast speaking)
4. Irritable mood, poor attention
5. Insomnia, increased appetite.
6. Loss of inhibitions: sexual, money, risk-taking

# Difference vs Hypomania
1. Has psychotic symptoms (Hypomania doesn't)
	1. Delusion of grandeur
	2. Auditory hallucinations
2. Elevated mood for at least 1 week.
3. Risk of harm to self or others- may need hospitalization.
