Inducers cause an increased risk of PCT toxicity if overdosed.
Even after stopping inducers, female still needs to use safe contraceptive method for additional 4-8 weeks.

# Avoid or co-prescribe list
## STATINS
### Simvastatin + clarithromycin / erythromycin
- ⛔ Do not co-prescribe
- Risk: Rhabdomyolysis
- ✅ Action: Stop simvastatin temporarily or switch antibiotic
### Simvastatin + amlodipine
- ⚠️ Max simvastatin = 20 mg
- If stronger statin needed → **switch to atorvastatin or rosuvastatin**

---
## WARFARIN
### Warfarin + metronidazole / clarithromycin/ TMP-SMX
- ⛔ Major INR rise → bleeding
- ✅ Action: avoid or very close INR monitoring / dose reduction
### Warfarin + NSAIDs
- ⛔ Bleeding risk (even if INR “normal”)
- ❗ Don’t reassure yourself with INR alone

---
## ACE-I / ARB Combos
### ACE-I / ARB + NSAID + diuretic
👉 “[[nsaid|Triple whammy]]”
- Risk: Acute kidney injury
- Common scenario: ramipril + bendro + ibuprofen
- ✅ Action: avoid NSAIDs, check U&Es if unavoidable
### ACE-I / ARB + potassium-sparing diuretic
- Ramipril + spironolactone
- Risk: [[hyperkalemia]], especially if CKD
- ✅ Action: monitor K⁺ closely 

---
## SSRIs & MENTAL HEALTH
### SSRI + NSAID
- Risk: GI bleed
- Very common oversight
- ✅ Consider PPI if unavoidable
### SSRI + tramadol
- ⛔ [[fever caused by drugs|Serotonin syndrome]] + seizures
- ✅ Use alternative analgesic
### SSRI + MAOI / linezolid
- ⛔ **Serotonin syndrome**
- Red flag combo 

---
## RESPIRATORY / ALLERGY 
### Non-selective β-blocker + asthma 
- Propranolol + asthma
- ⛔ Bronchospasm
- ✅ Use cardio-selective (bisoprolol) if essential 

---
## ANTIBIOTIC PITFALLS
### Clarithromycin + colchicine
- ⛔ Fatal toxicity risk
- Often forgotten
- ✅ Avoid or stop colchicine
### Nitrofurantoin + eGFR <45
- ⚠️ Reduced efficacy & toxicity 
- [BNF](https://bnf.nice.org.uk/drugs/nitrofurantoin/#renal-impairment): May be used with caution if eGFR 30–44 for short course only (3-7 days)

---
## PAIN & OPIOIDS
### Codeine / tramadol + fluoxetine / paroxetine
- Reduced analgesia (CYP2D6 inhibition)
- Plus ↑ serotonin syndrome risk with tramadol

### Opioids + benzodiazepines
- ⛔ Respiratory depression
- MHRA warning
- Very common in real life
---
## GOUT / IMMUNOSUPPRESSION
### [[allopurinol]] + azathioprine
- ⛔ Bone marrow suppression
- ✅ Azathioprine dose ↓ drastically or avoid 
---
## CONTRACEPTION 
### Combined oral contraceptive + rifampicin
- ⛔ Contraceptive failure
- Rifampicin induces enzymes
- ✅ Use alternative contraception

---
## DIABETES
### Insulin / sulfonylurea + quinolones
- Risk: **Hypoglycaemia**
- Elderly especially vulnerable
