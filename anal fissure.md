Anal fissure can cause fresh rectal bleed when patient passes stool. Examination shows a longitudinal tear occurring posteriorly and in the midline.

Don't do DRE as painful. 

The presence of a lateral anal fissure is unusual and requires further investigation. If patient has anal ulceration -> suspicious of anal cancer and therefore requires a 2-week wait referral to colorectal surgeons.
