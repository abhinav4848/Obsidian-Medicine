**Difflam spray** aka Benzydamine hydrochloride mouthwash or spray may be useful in reducing the discomfort associated with a painful mouth that may occur at the end of life.

**Chlorhexidine mouthwash** is indicated if aphthous ulcers, gingivitis, or oral candidiasis were present.

**Choline salicylate gel** is used for aphthous ulcers and cold sores. It is available over the counter.

**Nystatin oral suspension** is a treatment for oral candidiasis.  It is important to check for oral candidiasis in patients with mouth pain, particularly palliative patients
