# NICE guidelines
NRT, varenicline or bupropion should not be favoured one over the other. 
They should be given as part of a plan to stop smoking on or before a particular date. 
- supply should last until 2 weeks after target stopping date. 
- if giving NRT, choose target date 2 weeks after starting NRT, else with others, choose 3-4 weeks. 
- further prescription only if quit attempt continuing.
- if not successful with them. don't repeat prescription for 6 months. 
- Do not offer NRT (nicotine replacement therapy), varenicline or bupropion in any combination.

# NRT


# Varenicline
- **Varenicline**: Nicotinic receptor partial agonist. Varenicline works by partially activating the nicotinic acetylcholine receptor, reducing cravings for nicotine and withdrawal symptoms.


# Bupropion
**Bupropion**: Norepinephrine and dopamine reuptake inhibitor, and nicotinic acetylcholine antagonist.
