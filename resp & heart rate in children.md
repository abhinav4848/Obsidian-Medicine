Tachypnoea: respiratory rate  
- >50 breaths/minute, age 6-12 months;
- >40 breaths/minute, age >12 months

Tachycardia:
- >160 beats/minute, age <12 months
- >150 beats/minute, age 12-24 months
- >140 beats/minute, age 2-5 years
